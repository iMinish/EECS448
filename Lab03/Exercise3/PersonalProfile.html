The University of Kansas

Biography

The University of Kansas is the greatest university in the state of Kansas and located in one of the best cities in the world in Lawrence. Beyond the Lawrence campus, KU has several other academic campuses located in Kansas City and Overland Park. The Kansas Jayhawks belong to the Big 12 and are represented by their exceptional student-athletes as well as their beloved mascots Big Jay and Baby Jay. In 2008 the Kansas Jayhawks won the NCAA Men's Division I Basketball Tournament for the fifth time. Rock Chalk Jayhawk!
